# Bandit 10
Bandit 10 command to login to ssh with username: bandit10, password: <from Bandit 9>, and port 2220 -> 

**ssh -l bandit10 -p 2220 bandit.labs.overthewire.org**

Bandit 10 to Bandit 11 command to find and read contents of data.txt which is base64 encoded: **base64 -d data.txt** (-d means to decode the base64 encoded file)
