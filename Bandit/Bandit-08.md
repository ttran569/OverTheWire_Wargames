# Bandit 8
Bandit 8 command to login to ssh with username: bandit8 and port 2220 -> 

**ssh -l bandit8 -p 2220 bandit.labs.overthewire.org**

Bandit 8 to Bandit 9 command to find and read contents of data.txt with a line of text occurring once: **sort data.txt | uniq -u** (sort sorts the lines alphabetically, uniq -u outputs unique lines; sort is required for uniq -u to work because it only works on adjacent duplicates)
